Our Story

For over 30 years, our family has been perfecting the art of halwa making. Using recipes passed down through generations, we create authentic, delicious halwa that brings joy to every celebration.

We use only the finest ingredients - pure ghee, premium nuts, and traditional methods to ensure each bite is a taste of tradition.
